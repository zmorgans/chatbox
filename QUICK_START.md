# AI Debate Partner – Quick-Start Guide

Welcome to **AI Debate Partner**!  
This guide will help you understand what the app does and walk you through your first debate in just a few minutes.
## What This App Does

AI Debate Partner lets you practice real debate skills by arguing against an AI opponent. You choose the topic, your stance, and the AI persona—then move through **three rounds**: Opening, Rebuttal, and Closing. When you're done, the AI gives you clear feedback to help you improve. You don’t need any debate experience!

## Get Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Open the Settings Page

- Go to **Settings** in the app.  
- When prompted, enter the password: **debate123**.  
- Paste in your **Gemini API key** (you can get one for free through Google AI Studio).

Once saved, you’re ready to start your first debate.

---

## Try Your First Debate (Step-By-Step)

### Feature 1: Start a New Debate

1. On the **Home** page, type in a debate topic.  
   - Example: *"Should schools require uniforms?"*
2. Choose your stance: **Pro**, **Con**, or **Neutral**.
3. Pick an AI persona (e.g., *Academic Scholar*, *Investigative Journalist*, etc.).
4. Click **Start Debate**.

You will now enter the 3-round structure:

- **Round 1:** Opening Statements  
- **Round 2:** Rebuttals  
- **Round 3:** Closing Arguments  

Simply write your response for each round, then read the AI’s reply.

---

### Feature 2: Receive Instant Feedback

After all three rounds, the app will generate a performance report and score your performance using these categories:

- **Clarity**
- **Logic**
- **Evidence**
- **Structure**

You’ll also get personalized tips for improvement.

This is designed to make you more confident with each debate.

---

### Feature 3: Save or Export Your Debate

When your debate ends, you have two options:

#### Save
Click **Save Debate** to add the full transcript to your personal debate history. You can also revisit past debates anytime to review your arguments, track your progress, and compare scores across different debates.

#### Export
Click **Export as JSON** to download the debate for use in essays, study notes, or portfolio work.
