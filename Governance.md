# AI Usage Strategy

- **Ad-hoc Assistance**: Brainstorming feature ideas, drafting documentation, debugging snippets, suggesting test cases.
- **Systematic Automation**: Generating unit tests, summarizing meeting notes, creating first drafts of repetitive documents.

### Collaboration Protocol

- AI is treated as a **supporting collaborator**, not an authority.
- AI outputs must be **reviewed, fact-checked, and adapted** before inclusion.

- **Default**: Consensus among all four members.
- **If no consensus**: The member whose role is most relevant makes the final decision  
  _e.g., Technical Lead for architecture, Design Lead for UX._
- **Escalation**: Project Lead facilitates a short discussion and calls for a majority vote.
