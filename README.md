Debate is one of the most effective ways to build persuasive communication and critical reasoning skills. However, opportunities to practice are limited:  
- Students often struggle to find debate partners.  
- Structured practice sessions require significant scheduling.

---

## ğŸ’¡ Our Solution
The **AI Debate Partner** is a web-based platform that provides students with an instant, on-demand debate partner. Users can:  
- Choose a topic and stance.  
- Debate against the AI, which automatically argues the opposite side.  
- Experience structured rounds (openings, rebuttals, closings).  
- Receive AI-powered feedback on clarity, logic, and evidence.  
- Export transcripts for later review and reflection.
